About

Global Investors Pty Ltd (Global) is part of the Muhlbauer Group.

While focused on early stage venture capital, Global seeks opportunities across a variety of asset classes including listed equities, currencies, commodities, crypto and mezzanine finance.

The team consists of Phillip Muhlbauer (Principal), Daniel O'Malley (Investment Analyst) and a talent pool of the brightest and most motivated interns.

Philosophy

Our goal is to achieve alpha by investing or trading in special situations.

In the venture capital space we are passionate about backing great talent and bringing ideas to life. We achieve this by providing direct investment and strategic guidance to early stage companies and their founders.
